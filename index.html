SUNDARAM TIWARI

Welcome to Tandoori Adda

At Tandoori Adda, we bring the essence of traditional Indian tandoori flavors to your plate with a modern touch. Our passion for food is reflected in every dish, crafted with fresh ingredients and authentic spices to deliver an unforgettable dining experience.

  • Authentic Taste – Rich and smoky tandoori dishes with time-honored techniques.
  • Quality Ingredients – Fresh and high-quality ingredients for unforgettable flavors.
  • Warm Hospitality – A cozy ambiance that makes every meal a celebration.

From sizzling kebabs to buttery naans, every bite at Tandoori Adda is a journey into India's vibrant culinary heritage.

ROHIT TRIPATHI

Meet the Owners

Tandoori Adda was founded by **Sundaram Tiwari** and **Rohit Tripathi**, two passionate entrepreneurs with a shared love for authentic Indian cuisine. Their vision is to bring traditional tandoori flavors to modern diners, ensuring each dish delivers a taste of India's rich culinary heritage. With years of experience and a dedication to quality, they strive to make every meal a memorable one.

Paneer Tikka

Cottage cheese marinated in rich spices and grilled to perfection.

Served with mint chutney, this smoky and flavorful dish is an all-time favorite.

Dal Makhani

A rich, buttery lentil dish slow-cooked for ultimate flavor.

Best paired with fresh naan or kulcha, this dish is a treat for every vegetarian food lover.

Garlic Naan

Soft naan infused with aromatic garlic butter.

A perfect companion for all gravies and curries, baked to fluffy perfection.

Stuffed Kulcha

Kulcha stuffed with spiced mashed potatoes and herbs.

Served hot with butter and paired best with chole or dal makhani.

Pudina Paratha

Whole wheat paratha layered with fresh mint leaves.

Light and flaky, this paratha enhances the flavors of every meal.
